## English (Meaning Translation)

**Sikh Educational Conference**
Coming back from there, **S. Sundar Singh Ji Majithia**, who was in that Jatha, held a select gathering of Panth on **9 January 1908** at his residence...
In which gentlemen from Lahore, Amritsar and Gujranwala marking their presence...
proceedings took place under the presidentship of **Sardar Tarlochan Singh Ji**, **Bhai Jodh Singh Ji** was appointed Secretary.
Then after holding one or two more meetings, the foundation of the Conference was laid with consensus and it was decided to hold the first conference at **Gujranwala**. And it was held there on **17-18-19 April 1908**.

In the issue of **23-9-26** of 'Khalsa Samachar', printed 3 weeks after Bhai Hira Singh Ji's passing, the kind editor started the main article with these lines:
"The picture of religious Diwans of the time of Conference comes before eyes. Anand...
Exactly such was his relation with the Conference, as if one's name [is associated with] the other...
[The Diwan], which used to be the main part of the Conference, used to be such a gathering that attracted Sangats the most and in this Bhai Hira Singh Ji's [role] was [vital]. In the newspapers, the call given to reach the Conference... generally the map of religious Diwan was drawn to attract Sangats...
For example, to reach the Fourth Conference at **Sialkot**, in 'Khalsa Samachar' of **7 March 1911**..."
