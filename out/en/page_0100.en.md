## English (Meaning Translation)

'...is pure, is immaculate. Keep soul higher than body. Do not worry about bodily pain. This pain is in body but soul is healthy. True King wants to take you in His lap by liberating you from the cycle of birth and death in this birth itself...'
*Pivat Ram Ras Amrit Pivau Sila Bikh Jivau ||*
*Har Ka Naam Sada Nidh Nihchal Eh Dhan Jate Kau Devo ||*
(Guru Granth Sahib, Ang 496)

Interacting like this, Sant Ji departed from there. Bhai Sahib's heart got cooled, consciousness became high and soul peaceful.

**Sant Baba Nand Singh Ji (1879-1943):**
Who was an image of happiness and love, practiced, respected and preached his main ideal: accepting Sri Guru Granth Sahib Ji as Guru. Due to detachment (Upramta) and Vairag from young age, he did not marry. Blessed many places of Western Punjab and jungles with his touch. Left the arrangement of food and water to God and lived...
Bhai Hira Singh Ji's meeting with Baba Ji seems to have happened in **1920-21**. His younger brother, **Bhai Pardhan Singh Ji** has mentioned this small meeting in these words:
"Bhai Sahib went and met **Jagraon Wale Sant Baba Nand Singh Ji** at about midnight and folding hands requested, 'O Guru Beloved Gurmukh, *Deh Sajan Asisriyan Jiyo Hovai Sahib Sio Mel*'. Regarding this meeting whatever words happened between Bhai Sahib and Baba Ji, Bhai Sahib did not tell, but said this much: 'Sitting in this pleasure, it became almost Amrit Vela, time for Kirtan.' So Bhai Sahib Ji performed Kirtan of Asa Di Var, whose Bhog fell at sunrise. Upon Bhog, Sant Baba Ji said to Bhai Hira Singh Ji, 'What offering should I make for this Kirtan? You have blessed me with that thing, equal to which there is no material object on earth. Kirtan is Priceless Diamond (Nirmolak Heera)...'"
*(Guru Granth Sahib, Ang 815)*

**Bhai Sahib Bhai Sudh Singh Ji**, with whom Bhai Hira Singh Ji had extreme love, was a great spiritual rasia, melodious Kirtaniya and sweet explainer. Living an age of about 90 years, he [passed away] on **13 November 1972**...

## Notes
- Meeting with the great Saint **Baba Nand Singh Ji** of Jagraon (Nanaksar).
- Baba Nand Singh Ji's appreciation of Bhai Hira Singh Ji's Kirtan as "Nirmolak Heera".
- Mention of Bhai Sudh Singh Ji's passing in 1972.
