**Reaching Dehradun:**
Even before the family's arrival, the plan for Bhai Sahib to go to Dehradun had been made. The beloved Gurmukh, Dr. Balbir Singh Ji, had arranged for radium treatment with an English doctor there and had written a letter. So, on August 15, 1926, Bhai Sahib was sent to Dehradun.
The Hakim (traditional healer) had already stopped treatment continuously for several days prior, but a day before his departure, he came to meet Bhai Sahib Ji and met him with great love.
In Dehradun, he stayed in a bungalow (Kothi) on Rajpur Road, which was close to that English doctor's hospital. All this arrangement had already been done by Dr. Balbir Singh Ji. After a few days, Bhai Sahib forbade further treatment and said, "I have read only Gurbani, [which says]..." [implying acceptance of death].

About a week later, Manager Bhai Sham Singh Ji and Bapu Ji arrived to ask about his well-being. After staying for two-three days, they returned to Firuka, and Bhai Pradhan Singh Ji also went with them, as news of a child's illness had arrived from home.

**Departing for Gurpuri (Passing Away):**
At the time of departing from Kasur, Bhai Hira Singh Ji had expressed his wish to Bhai Sudh Singh Ji: "At my final time, you must definitely be near me." Bhai Sahib's Ardas was accepted at the door of the Giver Guru, and the Satguru arranged such a time that on August 30, 1926, Bhai Sudh Singh Ji arrived in Dehradun. Seeing him, [Bhai Hira] Singh Ji was very pleased and said, "Come Bhai Sahib, welcome. I was waiting for you. You have come, the Giver has been very kind. Now perform Ardas that my end comes [peacefully?]."
