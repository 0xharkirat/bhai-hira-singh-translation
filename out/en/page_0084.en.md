## English (Meaning Translation)

*Tudh Aage Ardas Hamari Jiyo Pind Sabh Tera ||*
...
*Guru Nanak Sabh Teri Wudiyayi Koi Nao Na Janai Mera ||*

[Someone] said, 'Bhupinder Singh's greatest happiness is in this matter... that he...
should do maximum service of Panth and not bring any pride or glory to self. I consider the treasury and rule of Patiala as trust of Akal Purakh...'

*Kabir Mera Mujh Meh Kichh Nahi Jo Kichh Hai So Tera ||*
(Kabir, nothing is mine within me. Whatever there is, is Yours.)
(Guru Granth Sahib, Ang 1375)

Finally he asked this demand from Panth that "Pray that Satguru may accept me as humble servant of His house...".
Two thousand Namdhari Sikhs came forming a Jatha. **Chief Khalsa Diwan's** Jatha, under the leadership of **Bhai Hira Singh Ji** and **Sant Sangat Singh Ji** [participated] in Sri Darbar [Sahib]...

**Jaito Morcha:**
This started from agitation against the deposition of **Maharaja Ripudaman Singh** of Nabha (9 July 1923). On 9 September 1923, Panthic leaders announced to celebrate 'Nabha Day' and on this day Akhand Path was kept at Gurdwara Gangsar. On **14 September 1923**, Government employees forcefully interrupted the Path by lifting the reciters. This caused resentment in Sangat... arrested everyone. Agitation increased further.
[Jathas] started going after taking pledge and this series continued till January 1924. [Total] 5 thousand men were imprisoned like this. On 9 February first **Shahidi Jatha** of 500 [marched]...

## Notes
- "Jaito Morcha" history.
- Maharaja Bhupinder Singh's humility during Kar Sewa is highlighted.
- Maharaja Ripudaman Singh of Nabha was deposed by British for his pro-Akali stance.
