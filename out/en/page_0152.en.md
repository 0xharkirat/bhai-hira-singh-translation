**Articles Published on Bhai Hira Singh Ji**
(From 1926 till now, hundreds of articles about Bhai Hira Singh Ji have been published in newspapers and magazines. The summary of some selected articles from them is recorded below:)

**Ah! Bhai Hira Singh Ji Ragi 'Kirt-Murti' (Embodiment of Praise)**
*From the main article of Khalsa Samachar, September 9, 1926*

Bhai Hira Singh Ji Ragi was among those special people who are not born always. Rather, special national and country needs produce them...
There is not even a grain of doubt in this matter that in the last 25 years, the amount of work this lone General did—by pulling souls out of the pit of atheism, faithlessness, and vices, and making them pure and holy, seating them in the Guru's lap through his heart-attracting speeches—no single man has done in this time.

**Empty Space in the Panth**
*Sriman Bhai Sahib Bhai Vir Singh Ji,*
*Main Article, Khalsa Samachar, September 30, 1926*

Almost all Sikh newspapers are crying in sorrow over the demise of Bhai Hira Singh Ji. Numerous Jathas, organizations, and Ashrams [are feeling] the lack of this one individual.
[Many] people have felt his demise as an unbearable loss to the Panth, which [they] have never done for anyone in the last forty years. Outside the Sikh circle, in the Hindu, Muslim, Christian, Arya public, whoever has ever heard his Kirtan or exposition, has sighed in pain for them.
