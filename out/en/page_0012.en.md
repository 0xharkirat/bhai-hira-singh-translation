## English (Meaning Translation)

Our most respected father, Major Balwant Singh Ji, completed his worldly journey of [number] years on 7 May 2004 and departed for the Guru's Abode. He had a special love for this book. His younger brother, Major Agyapal Singh Ji, performed great service in reaching this book to the Sikh Sangats. He too had passed away in June 1999.

According to the desire of the family, a function is organized every year in the memory of Bhai Hira Singh Ji. In this, there has often been a demand that [this book be reprinted].
[Therefore], an effort has been made to print the book for the third time with [some] changes.

1138 Sector 33-C,                            **Dasre** (Servants/Humble),
1 September 2009                             (Chairman, Bhai Hira Singh Memorial Trust)
