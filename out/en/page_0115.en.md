## English (Meaning Translation)

**Padi Darveza is 8 miles from Sohawa Station.**

**Influence on an Arya Samaj Lawyer (Pindi Gheb):**
School... became such target of jealousy and malice that School was almost in jaws of death. In city propaganda of Arya Samaj increased and a Lawyer Sahib who was graduate of D.A.V. College, started preparation to open **Arya School** and opposed **Khalsa School** fully.
Coincidentally, school managers met Bhai Sahib Bhai Hira Singh Ji in **Rawalpindi**. Telling him the condition of school, they requested his visit. Hearing the story of school, he sighed and reached **Pindi Gheb**.
It was Sunday. Special invitation was given to Hindu Arya lawyers and Muslim officers of the city... That Lawyer also came and was seated in front of Bhai Sahib Ji.
Kirtan [by] Bhai Hira Singh Ji... explanation by a scholar like **Vachaspati**. Such color solidified that Lawyer Sahib became emotional. Karuna Ras (Pathos) overwhelmed him and tears started falling from eyes uncontrollably...
Diwan ended. Lawyer Sahib reached home. After some time his neighbor, a **Sehajdhari Sikh**, met him. Asked, 'Tell, what are the thoughts after listening to Kirtan?' Lawyer Ji looking at Sehajdhari neighbor said, 'The taste and bliss experienced today, I had never seen in whole life. I wish! Time of Kirtan had not ended today.'
Bhai Sahib performed Kirtan for two more days and this Lawyer Ji went to listen on both days. Result was that question of opening Arya School completely receded... and [he] continued giving appropriate help to [Khalsa School].

**(Khalsa Samachar, 23 September 1926 Main Article):**
...used to get enamored. Flood of Vairag used to flow. His tongue...
...could not move from Diwan before [conclusion].
His great miracle was seen in **Sahiwal** Diwan. Morning... [Bhai Hira Singh] Bhag Singh Ji were performing Asa Di Var Kirtan...

- **Pindi Gheb Incident**: An Arya Samaj lawyer, initially opposed to Khalsa School, was transformed by Bhai Hira Singh's Kirtan. He abandoned his plan to open a rival Arya School and supported the Khalsa School instead.
- **Sahiwal Incident**: Intro to another miracle/event in Sahiwal.
