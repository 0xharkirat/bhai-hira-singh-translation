## English (Meaning Translation)

[...Previous text cut off] ...found his other diaries, which were up to 1925. In these, [there was] much...

Taking the aforementioned register and diaries, I appeared in the presence of Bhai Sahib Bhai Vir Singh Ji. He handed over all that material to Dr. Balbir Singh Ji. Some time later, Doctor Sahib called me and said, "Clean [this]... and organize it under different subject headings."

Due to the struggle for livelihood, I could not do this. [I said], "I have kept the daily journal and the news written from the diaries safe with me... This is the property of the Panth; hand it over to us so that the life of this unique servant of the Panth can be recorded." So Mata Ji handed over that register and the bundle of [diaries] to Bhai Sudh Singh Ji. This is perhaps a mention of 1944.

But God's will was such that during the partition of the country, this entire bundle remained at Bhai Sudh Singh Ji's house in Sargodha. [It was lost].

Then, on the occasion of the 25th death anniversary of Bhai Hira Singh Ji, Sriman Bhai Sahib Bhai Vir Singh Ji published a special [issue] of 'Khalsa Samachar' on 30 August 1951. Many loved ones sent their articles and poems. These articles were so numerous [that] another special issue of 'Khalsa Samachar' was published on 28 August 1952...

Since then, almost every year on Bhai Sahib's death anniversary, articles by one or another Gurmukh have continued to be published. The largest share among these belongs to Bhai Sahib Bhai Sudh Singh Ji, who had immense love for Bhai Hira Singh Ji. Reading about the life events and Panthic services of Bhai Sahib in these articles, I began to feel the [value] of my heritage, and a desire arose to preserve this heritage!

[It was decided] that a complete biography should be recorded. We assured him of financial service... [but] his eyes and health did not support him.

In 1967, I had an occasion to go to the office of the Chief Khalsa Diwan in Sri Amritsar...
