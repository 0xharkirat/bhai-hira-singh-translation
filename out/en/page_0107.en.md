## English (Meaning Translation)

**Bhai Amar Singh Ji Nambardar of Gondal (1870-1926?):**
[Bhai Hira Singh Ji] had [love] with **Bhai Amar Singh Ji Gondal** (18??-1926) and used to do Satsang.
Their lands were in a village named **Gondal** and when Bhai Amar Singh Ji used to be on lands, then Bhai Hira Singh Ji especially used to go there covering distance...
Bhai Amar Singh Ji's life was like that of complete Gurmukh. Waking up at last watch of night (Pehla Pehar)... Nitnem and Bhajan...
At time of Parkash (opening) of Guru Granth Sahib, it was necessary for whole family (four sons and...) to be present there before taking Maharaj's Hukam.
He had deep love and sympathy for every being. *"Manas Ki Jaat Sabh Ekai Pehchanbo"*.
Once upon asking Bhai Amar Singh Ji the reason [for sadness?], he said, 'We have no place here now, because despite having majority we have been deprived of **Nambardari** (headmanship).' Hearing this Bhai Sahib was moved and inspired him to stay there only, and assured him that soon He will get **half Nambardari** registered in his name. Accepting his word he stayed back and within one year Bhai Amar Singh Ji got half Nambardari from Muslims.

- **Bhai Amar Singh Ji Gondal**: A devout Nambardar (village head). Bhai Hira Singh Ji predicted he would get Nambardari, which came true.
