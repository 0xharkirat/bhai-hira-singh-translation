## English (Meaning Translation)
...[Copy of Resolution?] is being given here.

**Executive Committee of Chief Khalsa Diwan**
*Resolution Number 3, Date 11 September 1926*

The sorrowful news of the passing away of the humble servant of the Panth, Bhai Sahib Bhai Hira Singh Ji Ragi, was announced. upon which it was ordered that sympathy be expressed on behalf of the Diwan to Bhai Sahib's family. Bhai Sahib's [presence?]...
...was not only for the welfare of his own family, but [was] a very essential organ for the work of preaching Gurmat in the [Panth?]. But whatever happens...
...everyone has to accept it as Sweet Will (Bhana). Keeping the progress of the Panth in view...
...the service that [you/he] did in the Panth and kept doing from the beginning, has been a great means in the [causes?] of the Diwan's progress, and the Diwan's Committee [appreciates] that...

It was also approved that in the memory of Bhai Sahib Ji, on behalf of the Diwan, at its [associated?] Ashrams, Orphanage Amritsar, Gharjakh, Gujranwala and Shikarpur and Khalsa Pracharak Vidyalaya Tarn Taran, Akhand Paths of Sri Guru Granth Sahib be organized.
And request be made to the Singh Sabhas and other Jathas related to the Diwan that...
...Diwan should [pray] for granting residence to his soul in His feet.
...Apart from this, Akhand Path [should be done?] in the circumambulation (Parikrema) of Sri Darbar Sahib...
...Seva of [Karah Prasad?] be done, for which date be fixed and other necessary [arrangements?]...
...Sardar Harbans Singh Ji himself... should request to give Darshan...

## Notes
- Official resolution by Chief Khalsa Diwan expressing condolences and organizing Akhand Paths in his memory.
