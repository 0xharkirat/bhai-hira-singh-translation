## English (Meaning Translation)

**17th Conference which was held in May 1926 in Lahore**, was the last [conference] for Bhai Hira Singh Ji. At that time **Dr. Hira Singh Ji** was under treatment in **Kasur** hospital and could not be present physically. But he sent a written message through his younger brother **Bhai Pardhan Singh Ji** to Lahore, instructing him to read this last message of his in the Conference. Hearing this message hearts were moved and when finally this Slok written [in it] was read:
*Pankhi Chale Disavri Kirpa Sufal Falant ||*
(The bird flies to foreign lands; by Grace, it bears good fruit.)
(Guru Granth Sahib, Ang 1376)
Then everyone's eyes became wet.

**Giani Sant Singh Kamalia**, who had read out this message, describing the glory of Kirtan in Guru's house, in very learned and loving words, expressed the virtue of accompanying those attached to Guru Nanak's hem and said: "Bhai Mardana was extremely dear to Guru Nanak Dev Ji. The reason for this as written in **Nanak Prakash**, is as follows:
[Poetry quote: He plays the Rebeck... pleasing to my mind...]
Therefore, it is the duty of all of us to support those attached to Guru Nanak Dev Ji...
**700 Sikhs** gave five hundred rupees each for this Conference and became Life Fellows...
Master, on this many gentlemen announced becoming Life Fellows right there!
This was his love with the Conference which lasted till the last breaths.

**18th Conference which was held in April 1927 in Rawalpindi**, after Bhai Sahib's departure to Sach Khand...
