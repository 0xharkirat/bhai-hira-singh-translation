## English (Meaning Translation)

...dispute arose and they filed a suit against Bhai Amar Singh Ji. The case went from lower courts to **High Court**. In papers their [Bhai Amar Singh's] side was weak and contest was tough.
Bhai Amar Singh Ji was a Sidaki Sikh and had great faith in Guru House but this had become a question of honor. So he told his mind's pain to Bhai Hira Singh Ji. Bhai Sahib gave courage and recited this line:
*Tu Kahe Dole Paraniya Tudh Rakhaiga Sirjanhar ||*
(Why do you waver, O mortal? Create will protect you.)
And giving other proofs from Gurbani brought him in Chardi Kala and said:
*Jis Ka Sahib Dhada Hoi || Tis No Mar Na Sakai Koi ||*
(He closely on whose side is the Lord... no one can kill him.)
(Guru Granth Sahib, Ang 842)
Let us give... Our Privy Council is Satguru's Court, so:
*Upav Chhod Gahu Tis Ki Ote || Nimakh Mahe Hovai Teri Chhot ||*
(Leave other efforts and grasp His Support; in an instant you will be released.)
(Guru Granth Sahib, Ang 177)
"Therefore come, let us hold Akhand Path of Sri Guru Granth Sahib Ji and Ardas at Satguru's feet... whatever outcome comes from it... give to **High School Faruka**. With its income, poor and orphan children will get help in education and Langar." Bhai Amar Singh Ji accepted this proposal. So Akhand Path was held. On the day of Bhog many students of school reached there. **Bhai Bhag Singh Hira Singh Ji** performed Divine Kirtan of Asa Di Var...
*Prabh Jio Khasmana Kar Pyare || Bure Bhale Ham Thare ||*
(O Beloved Lord, make me Your own; good or bad we are Yours.)
(Guru Granth Sahib, Ang 631)
*Mere Har Jio Sabh Ko Terai Vas ||*
(My Dear Lord, everything is in Your control...)
(Guru Granth Sahib, Ang 736)

On the other side, after some time, it was the first day of **High Court** [being upgraded/renamed from Chief Court?] and first case. Bhai Amar Singh Ji's lawyer with great wisdom...

## Notes
- A legal dispute over land where Bhai Amar Singh's side was weak.
- Bhai Hira Singh Ji advised spiritual recourse: Akhand Path and Ardas, pledging the outcome to **High School Faruka**.
