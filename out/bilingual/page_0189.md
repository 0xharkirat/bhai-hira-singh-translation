## English (Meaning Translation)
...There was no one [like him].
His birth [was in] 1902 AD and demise happened on 16 November 1966.
...

**Bhai Samund Singh Ji**
He had such knowledge of Sur Gian (Music/Notes) that he could sing every Raga used in Guru Granth Sahib. He passed away in January 1972 at the age of 68.
