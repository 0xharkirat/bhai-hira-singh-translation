## English (Meaning Translation)

...[letter written by] Bhai Hira Singh in pain-filled words, reading which tears flow spontaneously... tried and warned the Panth many times. For example, a note published in **Khalsa Samachar** dated **7 October 1927** is as follows:
"**Priceless Diamond:** It has been only a few days that unique preacher of Sikh Panth **Bhai Hira Singh Ji Ragi** has passed away. Friend and foe alike are praising his capability with one voice and it is an accepted fact that the amount of Sikh Dharma preaching done by this solitary life, no single person has done in last 50 years... The question of establishing his memorial has come before Sikh public many times in one form or other, but till now no scheme seems to have matured. Appreciation of Arya Samaj for its servants and Khalsa Ji's..."
On the **25th Death Anniversary** of Bhai Hira Singh Ji, in the lead article of **Khalsa Samachar Special Issue (30 August 1951)** also, expressing a sweet complaint, Shriman Bhai Sahib Ji wrote:
"Today 25 years have passed since Bhai Hira Singh Ji Ragi went to the other world, as if an era has passed, but memory is still alive. Although the Panth whose unbroken service he did, has not established any memorial for him, but in hearts..."
[Bhai Sahib Bhai Vir Singh Ji] gave [some amount?] for 'Scholarship' to **Khalsa College Amritsar** and ordered that with the interest of this amount, scholarships should be given to Amritdhari and Kirtan performing students.
In **1967**, sons of Bhai Hira Singh Ji offered similar amount more and doubled the scholarships.

**Bhai Hira Singh Ji's...**
...kept taking care of his consciousness. On this writer [Bhai Vir Singh?] he had special blessing.
(Letter)

## Notes
- Mentions the lack of a formal Panthic memorial for Bhai Hira Singh Ji despite his immense contribution.
- Bhai Vir Singh Ji established a scholarship in his memory at Khalsa College Amritsar.
