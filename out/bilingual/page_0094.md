## English (Meaning Translation)

...Poet Santokh Singh's devotion-filled verses, the way he glorified [Guru], feeling that from heart... [Bhai Hira Singh Ji] used to get absorbed in the juice of Satguru's praise and used to grant a particle of this juice to this slave (Bhai Vir Singh) also...

*Keh Ravidas Akath Katha Bahu Kahi Karijai ||*
*Jaisa Tu Taisa Tuhi Kia Upma Dijai ||*
(Says Ravidas, Your story is ineffable, what more can be said? You are like Yourself only, what comparison can be given?)
(Guru Granth Sahib, Ang 858)

...wrote soaked in love and Blessed Commentator Bhai Sahib...
Bhai Hira Singh Ji said in extreme love and humility, 'Bhai Sudh Singh, they will be blessed people who will see this Suraj Prakash complete and realized and will read it. May my Giver Satguru grant me long life, Bhai Sudh Singh, may you live long, [so that] you may express my keen desire and heart's wish in Guru Sangats... wherever... establish Gur-Pratap Suraj Granth under Maharaj's Peehra (throne) or in the lower shelf of cabinet. Along with Satguru's Holy Form, may they read Satguru's history also with enthusiasm and drink nectar of Guru's praise. The garden of Guru Baba's praise...'
(Guru Granth Sahib, Ang 1376)

**Bhai Sahib Bhai Vir Singh Ji's love for Bhai Hira Singh Ji:**
Where Bhai Hira Singh Ji had deep love and respect for Shriman Bhai Sahib Bhai Vir Singh Ji in his heart, there Sri Bhai Sahib Ji also had deep love and pain for Bhai Hira Singh Ji in his heart and this love lasted till the end of Bhai Hira Singh Ji's body.
On Bhai Hira Singh Ji becoming ill, he came twice to enquire about his health. First time, in **March 1926**, when he was staying at **Lahore** in the bungalow of **Sardar Bahadur Hari Singh Deputy Commissioner**, then Sri Bhai Sahib Ji met him with great love... and after sharing spiritual words when he started to leave, he asked, 'Any service for me, please tell?' Bhai Hira Singh Ji had horripilation (hair stood on end) and said, 'You already take much trouble. Now do one favor, please send **Bhai Sudh Singh Ji**...'

## Notes
- "Bhai Sudh Singh" - Associate/Assistant.
- Bhai Vir Singh Ji visited the ailing Bhai Hira Singh Ji twice, showing their close bond.
