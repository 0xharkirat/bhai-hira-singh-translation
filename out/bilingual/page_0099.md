## English (Meaning Translation)

...went to his bungalow. He was just away from the bungalow, that **Sardar [Vohra?]** from station...
Sant Ji along with Jatha reached straight to **Gurdwara Bangla Sahib** and started Asa Di Var Kirtan. Here Bhai Hira Singh Ji got information that Saints... have arrived. Bhai Sahib felt as if moon had risen, body and mind blossomed and coming in gratitude he got absorbed in Simran.

...threaded with own hands and offering it to Sri Guru Granth Sahib Ji, he kept one garland from them with himself. He remained engaged in recitation of Bani.
[Letter/Message]:
"His soul has such attraction that we are compelled to come here soon..."
**Bhai Pardhan Singh Ji**, who was sent by Bhai Hira Singh Ji to bring Saints from Bangla Sahib... was standing nearby. Placing head on Saints' feet [he said], 'Bhai Hira Singh Ji is also yearning for your Darshan...'
...Along with Jatha Sri Sant Ji reached Malik Sahib's bungalow.
...cars were parked towards the gate of the bungalow. Seeing Sant Ji... started reading:
*Amrit Neka Tan Haria Gur Peh Mo Ko Lavau ||*
*(Nectar rains, body becomes green, take me to the Guru)*
(Guru Granth Sahib, Ang 1018)

...folding hands, bowed head on [Sant Ji's] feet and taking him...
...garland... which was kept near... put in Sant Ji's neck...
[Spent] time in remembrance of Akal Purakh...

## Notes
- Describes the last emotional meeting between Bhai Hira Singh Ji and Sant Attar Singh Ji in Delhi (1926).
- Bhai Hira Singh Ji was ill and Sant Ji visited him.
