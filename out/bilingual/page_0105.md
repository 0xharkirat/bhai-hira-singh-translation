## English (Meaning Translation)

[Regarding Bhai Sant Singh Ji]:
...
**(Khalsa Samachar, 1966, written by S. Manohar Singh)**
On the passing away of Bhai Sant Singh Ji, Sachkhand-dweller Bhai Sudh Singh Ji wrote a letter to the bereaved family, in which refreshing the memory of his supreme love for Bhai Hira Singh Ji...
"Jio! Folding my hands, turning desires of mind, sitting with soul... cautious and one-pointed listener has come, Sultan of Beautiful ones... Court of... 'Tithey Bhagat Vasah Ke Loa'. In Bhai Sant Singh's [heart], wave of Kirtan has left..."

**Sardar Manmohan Singh Ji (Born 1893):**
Name practitioner (Naam Abhyasi) with high life...
Who got Satsang of Bhai Sahib Bhai Vir Singh Ji throughout life...
Always remained dyed in color of Naam-Bani.
...were residents of **Rawalpindi** or **Abbottabad**, but their [love] for Satsang was like...
Whenever [Bhai Hira Singh Ji came]... met him and got happy...
Later when S. Manmohan Singh Ji came to **Lahore**, he got more opportunity to meet.
When Bhai Sahib came to Lahore or Bhai Sahib went to Kasur... spiritual happiness was attained meeting him. Once he especially went to **Faruka** to visit... Meeting **Bhai Sant Singh Ji**...
And then spent [time] with Bhai Sahib Bhai Hira Singh Ji...
...For writing biography, Sardar Manmohan Singh gave... [help/material].

## Notes
- Mentions **S. Manmohan Singh Ji**, another close associate of Bhai Hira Singh Ji and Bhai Vir Singh Ji, who provided material for this biography.
- Refers to a tribute letter by Bhai Sudh Singh on death of Bhai Sant Singh.
