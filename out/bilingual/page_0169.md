## English (Meaning Translation)
**Then the Panth Needs You**
*'Khalsa Samachar' 28.8.1952*

...Your explanation with Kirtan puts life into the hearts...
Distributing the Nectar (Amrit), making the congregation intoxicated...
Joining [them] with Guru's Feet through sweet speech...

**The Soul of All Panthic Works**
*From an article in 'Khalsa Samachar' 28.8.1952*

Bhai Hira Singh Ji's name is famous in the Panth especially as a 'Ragi', but Bhai Sahib was not only a Ragi, rather, along with being a Ragi, he was the 'Soul' of all Panthic works. There was no such educational, religious and reformative Panthic movement in which Bhai Hira Singh Ji did not take part more than others with his body, mind and wealth. ...

**Showed by Becoming the Diamond (Hira) of the Community**
*'Khalsa Samachar', 28.5.1952*
