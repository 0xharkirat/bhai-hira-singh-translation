## English (Meaning Translation)

...[Bavan Akhri], Sidh Gosht etc. all had been memorized and I had also studied religious texts like Suraj Prakash...
...Baba Jiwan Singh Ji had bestowed... who [used to sit] in the lap...
They used to say, "...Recite the Paath of Sukhmani Sahib." My age was about 15 years then. Many times just to fix my mind... he used to order me to read three times...

Along with being a student of Bhai Mahna Singh Ji, he also remained a companion of Bapu Ji (Bhai Bhag Singh Ji) in Kirtan in the Dharamsala. Occasionally, he used to go on Kirtan tours towards **Pothohar** with Bapu Ji.

**Meeting with Nirankaris and Namdharis:**
During these very tours, he got a chance to go to **Rawalpindi**. Here at the Dera of **Baba Dyal Ji**, he obtained the company of Nirankari brothers. Then he met Namdharis at **Bhaini Sahib** and first took Amrit (initiation) here. From there the passion for practice (Abhyas) awoke, but when looking at the service of Sri Guru Granth Sahib... the question of the need for a Guru... mind got detached...

**Marriage:** According to the custom of the time, his marriage took place at a young age in **1894** with **Bhagan Bai** (who later became Singhni and was named **Prem Kaur**), the elder daughter of **Bhai Duni Chand** (who later became a Singh named Bhai Amar Singh Ji) of Faruka.
Hardly six months after the marriage, such an attraction of Satsang occurred that the love of body (worldly attachment) went away... and he started molding his life in Satsang and Service-Simran.

[While] Baba Ji was flowing the stream of Kirtan, such a divine effect happened on Bhai Hira Singh that a longing arose in his mind to stay in the feet of Saints... and take Amrit from the Guru. So when the Jatha... from Amritsar... requested... Bhai Hira Singh [asked] to join the Jatha in the feet of Saints.
He used to participate in Kirtan with the Jatha... Sants [saw]... pure throat and sweet voice experience, so Sant Ji...
In year **1895**... when the Jatha left... Bhai Hira Singh Ji went along with them. This time... Bhai Hira Singh Ji's age was about 16 years. Then approximately...
