He became a lover of Gurbani Kirtan...
The result came out that about 50 years ago, when [Bhai Hira Singh?]...
When [S.] Pal Singh Ji was posted in Calcutta, then [he] took up the service of teaching Kirtan. These Kirtan classes [produced?] trained scholars...
And in 1988, S. Narinder Singh Ji established Gurbani Music Academy.
In this Academy, no fee is charged, rather, needy children are also given instruments. Many Sindhis, Bengalis and other non-Sikhs have also learned and are learning Kirtan. These children, reading Gurbani, doing pure pronunciation... are doing service.
