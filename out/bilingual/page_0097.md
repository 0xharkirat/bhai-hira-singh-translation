## English (Meaning Translation)

**Ik Onkar Sri Waheguru Ji Ki Fateh ||**

Shriman Respected Bhai Sang Singh Jio,
Your letter has been received. The date for Kaka and Kaki's wedding is to be fixed...
Whatever Waheguru Ji does, is good. May True King Guru accomplish both tasks with happiness.
Many many congratulations from my side to you and children's mother.
In this envelope there are **two handkerchiefs** and **one cheque**. Please accept this for both tasks...
May the couples be long-lived and possessors of Sikh Sidak. May the hand of your love remain on children's heads. Congratulations, Dhan Guru Nanak Dev, Dhan Guru Nanak Dev.

## Notes
- A personal letter (likely from Bhai Hira Singh Ji or Bhai Vir Singh Ji) congratulating on a wedding and sending gifts. Content is brief.
