## English (Meaning Translation)

**Bhai Hira Singh Ji**
"Guru Nanak, You have bestowed this gift."

**Birth:** As we have already mentioned, Bhai Hira Singh Ji was born in the year **1879** at the house of Bhai Bhag Singh Ji with the blessings and prayer of Sant Bhai Ram Kishan Ji of Shahpur Dera. At the time when this beautiful child was born, a wave of happiness ran through the whole family and congratulations started coming from all sides. But [the greatest] happiness was to **Baba Jiwan Singh Ji** who had seen the face of a [grand]child in the house after full 35 years.

**Education:** He received primary education of Gurmukhi Gurbani in the Dharamsala of **Bhai Mahna Singh Ji**... But after a short while due to domestic hurdles the studies had to be stopped and worldly...
Gurmukh beloved **Bhai Ishar Singh Ji Khurana** used to tell that he was also a student of Bhai Mahna Singh Ji at that time. According to him - "Bhai Hira Singh Ji was very fond of reading Gurbani and Gur-History and he used to remain ahead of other students. Even in childhood his inclination was towards reading religious books.
Apart from acquiring the understanding of meanings of Guru Granth Sahib Ji, he also studied Granths like **Suraj Prakash**, **Gur Bilas** and **Varan Bhai Gurdas** etc.
He memorized a lot of Gurbani and in this task he kept getting encouragement and inspiration from the practical life of his grandfather **Baba Jiwan Singh Ji**.

In the year 1926, when this author used to recite the daily Nitnem and Paath of Baani to Bhai Sahib during his illness, one day he told me that, "At your age, apart from the Baanis of Nitnem, I had [memorized] Sukhmani Sahib and Bavan... [Akhri]..."
