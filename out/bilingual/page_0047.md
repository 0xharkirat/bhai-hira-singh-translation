## English (Meaning Translation)

Taking it to a popular position is the fruit of the labor of Singh Sabha servants.
In this aspect, the labor of **Bhai Hira Singh Ji** is significant. Bhai Hira Singh Ji's own marriage, which took place at a young age in **1894**, was also performed with Brahmanical rites. But when he came to Sri Amritsar in **1896** and came in contact with Singh Sabha servants, he presented himself at Sri Akal Takht and got his marriage performed again according to Gur-Maryada! Here itself he administered Amrit to his wife (Singhni) and kept her name **Prem Kaur**.
The service of Kirtan and Preaching he started later from **1897**.

Facing many difficulties and opposition, he not only did solid preaching of Gur-Maryada but also made this custom successful. Wherever Bhai Hira Singh Ji reached for Gurmat Anand Karaj, he would create such an impact that listening to his oratory, many families would pledge to follow Gur-Maryada. Some samples of such many incidents are given below.
It is a matter of year **1904** that Bhai Sahib reached **Mardan** in connection with preaching. Here **Doctor Diwan Singh Ji**, famous historian **Baba Prem Singh Ji** and **Sardar Sant Singh Ji** of Garhi Kapura etc. Guru's beloveds had established a beautiful Satsang Mandal. Here he had been performing Kirtan for only a few days when a unique incident happened.

About 12 miles from **Hoti Mardan**, right on the border of non-settled (Ghair-Ilaka, tribal) area, a small village existed, whose name in Pashto was **'Sara Cheena'** (Cold Spring). A poor devout Sikh **Bhai Visakha Singh** lived here and about 9-10 households were of other faiths also.
Two of [his sons?] were killed by the bullets of Pathans of non-settled area. Bhai Visakha Singh...

## Notes
- "Vaktrita" means Oratory/Speech.
- "Ghair-Ilaka" refers to the tribal frontier areas not fully under British administration.
